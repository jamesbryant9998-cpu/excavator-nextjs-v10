# Shanghai Lizhihang Machinery International Trade Co., Ltd. - Company Overview

## About Us
Shanghai Lizhihang Machinery International Trade Co., Ltd. is a leading international sales company specializing in used construction machinery and equipment. Located in Minhang District, Shanghai, our facility covers an area of 500 acres. We are a trusted supplier to a wide range of global customers, retailers, and agents, maintaining long-term and stable cooperative relationships.

## Core Capabilities
- **Industry Experience**: With over 30 years of deep involvement in the second-hand excavator industry, we offer unparalleled expertise and a comprehensive selection of machinery.
- **Product Range**: We carry more than 10 top brands and over 80 different models. Our main products include excavators, bulldozers, cranes, graders, rollers, forklifts, dump trucks, and wheel loaders.
- **Quality Assurance**: Our company is ISO 9001-2000 certified and has obtained the 3C certification, ensuring reliable mechanical workability and quality across our entire inventory.
- **Customer Service**: Our philosophy is "Customer First, Profit Second." We provide professional services from pre-sale to after-sales, supported by a warm service attitude and strict specifications.
